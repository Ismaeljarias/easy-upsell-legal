Before you reach out

Here are a few quick checks that resolve most common issues:

  • App permissions: Make sure Easy Upsell has been granted the necessary permissions during installation. Re-installing the app will re-trigger the permission flow.
  • Active offers: Ensure at least one upsell offer is active and correctly configured in the app dashboard.
  • Theme compatibility: Easy Upsell works with most Shopify themes. If you're using a heavily customized theme, reach out and we'll help verify compatibility.
  • Browser cache: If changes aren't reflecting in your storefront, try a hard refresh (Ctrl+Shift+R or Cmd+Shift+R) or test in a private/incognito window.

Response times

We respond to all support requests within 48 business hours (Monday–Friday, excluding public holidays). For urgent issues affecting your live store, please note "URGENT" in your email subject line and we will prioritize your request.
